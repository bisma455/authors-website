ABOUT HOLLY JACKSON

Holly Jackson is a British author known for her work in the young adult (YA) mystery genre. she gained recognition for her debut novel, "A Good Girl's Guide to Murder," which was first published in 2019. This book became popular among YA readers and was followed by a sequel, "Good Girl, Bad Blood," released in 2020. The series is characterized by its engaging mystery plots and relatable teenage protagonists. Holly Jackson's books have been praised for their suspenseful storytelling, well-drawn characters, and the way they explore themes such as justice, truth, and the impact of technology on modern investigations. These novels appeal to readers who enjoy thrilling mysteries with a contemporary YA twist. EVENTS

Jackson's debut novel A Good Girl's Guide to Murder was published in 2019, where it received the following accolades: Library Association's Amazing Audiobooks for Young Adults (2021)[3] Goodreads Choice Award Nominee for Young Adult Fiction (2020)[4] Shortlisted for the YA Book Prize (2020)[5] British Book Awards Children's Fiction Book Winner of the Year (2020)[5] Barnes and Noble Best Books of the Year (2020)[6] Jackson followed this up with 2 sequel novels and one prequel novella: Good Girl, Bad Blood (2020); As Good As Dead (2021); and Kill Joy (2021) respectively. Apart for Good Girl, Bad Blood, all books were published by Electric Monkey. Good Girl, Bad Blood was published by Delacorte Press, and was shortlisted for the YA Book Prize in 2021.[7] In 2022, Jackson released her first standalone novel called Five Survive, published in the UK by Electric Monkey and in the US by Delacorte Press.[8]
